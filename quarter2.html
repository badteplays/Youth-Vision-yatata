Quarter 2

In this quarter, you will be able to go through how our team brainstormed, on how we will execute our upcoming stream next quarter. This involves picking the appropriate streaming content for our topic, choosing an alternative streaming platform we are planning to stream on, identifying the tools and equipment that we will be needing, creating a stream outline and flow, and lastly, our promotional plan.

Our Streaming Goals

Our goal for the stream is to create a valuable and top-notch stream which attracts at least 70+ viewers within the realm of financial education and literacy. We want to maintain a 1-2 hour time frame of our stream to help encourage active participation and enhance the overall learning experience for our viewers. We aim to empower Individuals with the essential knowledge about our recent discoveries in our research. This will enable them to construct a more wide view of E-money and its effect on individuals.



Our Chosen Content Type


In Quarter 1, we decided to conduct an Audience Analysis to gain insights about people's preferred content type which includes a mixed questionnaire of closed and open-ended questions that would help us recognize the things we would like to know and that includes their preferred content type. Recalling the results of the Audience Analysis, 48% (18/37) majority of our target participants have chosen Educational Content as the most suitable content type to use for our stream.

The group has decided that the streaming content type that our group will choose based on the Audience Analysis is Educational Content because it is a highly efficient method of effectively communicating intricate information in a concise yet comprehensive manner. Moreover, it may offer the advantages of visually representing ideas and concepts, thereby enabling a deeper understanding and enhanced engagement with the audience.

Our Chosen Streaming Platform and Tools

After some deliberation, our streaming platform will be transferred from YouTube to Twitch as both of them are easily accessible but Twitch has more features available for the audience to interact with and for us to utilize. We also determined the tools that we would need without purchasing anything and that is by maximizing our available resources and in addition the things we should keep in mind. Let’s elaborate on those and their importance and how all of this will contribute to enhancing the viewing experience of our audience.


  • Devices (e.g. Computer, laptop, phone, tablet)
    • Camera (Good Quality)
      Explanation: Having a good camera on our device will be crucial because we want to make sure that our viewers are able to watch our stream in good quality seeing the people speaking.

    • Audio (Clear and Understandable)
      Explanation: It is essential to acknowledge that this particular aspect holds significance in a stream that's why our device must have a properly working microphone. As it will enable us to communicate with each other and the viewers with the utmost effectiveness.
  • Softwares
    • Streaming Software
      Explanation: We need streaming software to stream and record our video, without it, there wouldn’t be a video to start with. Speaking of streaming software we decided to utilize OBS Studio because it is free and open-source, cross-platform screencasting and streaming app. It is a versatile tool that can be used for a variety of purposes that we think are appropriate for us.

    • Noise Cancellation Software
      Explanation: Noise cancellation software removes unwanted background noise, like traffic or construction, ensuring viewers focus solely on our voice and content, boosting engagement and professionalism.
  • Visuals
    • Proper Lighting
      Explanation: Cameras need plenty of light to produce a quality image and set the right atmosphere. We will use proper lighting so that we can produce higher-quality videos and so that our audience can see us well and clearly with the use of proper lighting in the video.

    • Background
      Explanation: The reason why we listed the background is that we want to be sure that our background during the stream is not too distracting, and we want to make sure that the audience is able to focus on what we are saying, and of course we want our stream to be easy for our viewers to watch.
  • Presentation
    • Engaging and interactive commentary
      Explanation: Good commentary on YouTube videos is essential because it will help us engage our audience by providing context, and adding a personal touch to our content. It also makes it easier for viewers to relate to us, comprehend the goal of the video, and maintain their attention, all of which contribute to better overall channel growth and increased viewer retention.
