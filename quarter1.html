Quarter 1

Throughout this quarter, you will have the chance to examine the documentation of our journey, commencing from the initial stages up until the discovery of the requirement for streaming. This will involve the explanation and elaboration of concepts related to streaming, including the Definition of Streaming, Different Types of Streaming Content, the Platforms employed for streaming, an evaluation of Viewers for each streaming genre, viewer needs, and the attributes that contribute to a desirable stream. All of this will be founded on fundamental research, our learnings from this quarter, and the administration of a survey.



What is streaming?

Streaming allows you to indulge in the pleasure of watching videos or listening to music without the tedious task of having to download them in advance. In today's age, it can be compared to a contemporary version of enchantment! With just a simple click, you can instantly immerse yourself in your favorite show or groove to a catchy tune. This remarkable convenience brings along a multitude of benefits, particularly in the sense that it eradicates the need to patiently wait for the complete content to be stored on your device. It's almost like having a remarkably efficient method of exchanging information with others.

Types of Streaming Content

There are many types of streaming content available, there’s texts, audio, videos, and live videos. First of all, Video is Streaming such as Movies, TV Shows, User-Generated Content, and more, this can be found on YouTube, and Streaming Services such as Disney+ or Netflix. There’s Music Streaming, which can be found on Streaming Services such as YouTube, Spotify, and Soundcloud. Live Streaming is also another type of streaming content, but instead of pre-recorded sessions such as Video Streaming, and Music Streaming, this is done live in Real-time, and these types of streaming can be found on YouTube, Twitch, Kick, Facebook, and other services. All these types of streaming can be made with different types of Genres as well, It can be Cooking, Gaming, Tutorials, Art, Documentary, News, Fitness, Educational, Sports, etc.

Platforms used for streaming



YouTube

YouTube is the most used and most popular streaming platform out there right now being used by millions, if not billions of people all over the world, 500 hours worth of videos are being uploaded to YouTube every minute, and Most people use this as a Streaming Services, but also a Job because of Monetization.

Facebook

This Platform is Popular due to its large user base, and basically integrated streaming features, ways to make money, interactive tools, and help creators reach a wide audience. Facebook can be used for different types of content and is easily accessible on mobile devices.


Twitch

Twitch is a live streaming platform that allows users to watch and create their own live streams, users can stream anything they like as long as it's within Twitch's community guidelines. Twitch focuses on video games live streams, and includes broadcasts of esports and competitions in addition it also offers music broadcasts, creative content, and “in real life” streams.


Disney+

Disney+ is used as a streaming service because it offers a wide range and variety of Disney movies and shows, such as Shows and Movies made by Pixar, Star Wars, Marvel, and more. Making it popular for subscribers of the service who want to access Shows, or movies in one place, and in one click.


Netflix

Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of content. You can use this by paying a monthly subscription. Subscribers can access a vast library of movies, TV shows, and original content. It also enables users to stream content on a wide range of devices, including TVs, computers, tablets, and smartphones.
