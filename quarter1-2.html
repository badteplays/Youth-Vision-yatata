III. BELIEFS AND OPINIONS ON THE TOPIC


Here are the set of questions that our survey contains and the summary of our total respondents' outputs.

Do you think finance is important for you?
For them, Finance provides the foundation for organizing and understanding the flow of money, enabling us to make informed decisions about budgeting, spending, and saving. It serves as a discipline for managing money wisely, ultimately leading to financial stability and the pursuit of life goals. Whether it's running a household, achieving future financial objectives, or simply finding happiness through wise financial choices, finance is a vital tool. It empowers individuals, particularly young adults transitioning into adulthood, to navigate the complexities of modern life.
Do you believe that e-money will eventually replace traditional currencies altogether?
Based on its inherent ease, improved security, and operational efficiency, some people claim that the transition to e-money is an inevitable trend. They argue that a growing proportion of the population will favor digital payment methods as long as technical development continues. A different group, however, expresses worries, highlighting issues including limited internet access, vulnerability to cyber threats and fraud, and the ongoing preference for physical money, particularly among small businesses.
Are you open to using e-money in your daily financial transactions?

They hold the belief that e-money can alleviate the inconveniences associated with carrying physical cash, rendering transactions swifter and more efficient. Furthermore, they express an appreciation for the enhanced security offered by e-money in the realm of financial transactions. Conversely, there exists a cohort of individuals who exhibit hesitancy towards e-money adoption, citing diverse factors such as their level of technological familiarity and reservations pertaining to impulsive spending tendencies. The acceptance and integration of e-money into one's financial practices appear to be contingent upon individual proclivities and specific life circumstances.

What do you think are the potential benefits and drawbacks of using e-money?
According to various opinions, Firstly, it reduces waste by promoting cashless transactions, reducing the need for paper currency. Secondly, It also enhances safety as it minimizes the risks associated with carrying physical cash. Lastly, e-money is praised for its convenience, allowing for quick and easy transactions, including bill payments. There are also drawbacks to consider. An issue as not all businesses and individuals have embraced e-money, limiting its usability in certain situations.
Do you think that e-money is worth the potential risks? Why?
Some individuals highlight the benefits of convenience and security, asserting that it’s a practical choice for modern transactions. They emphasize the role of trusted agencies in providing safety measures and believe that, when used cautiously, the advantages of e-money outweigh the risks, particularly for smaller transactions.
